    Cakerie Stall

    Customers sometimes face difficulty of choosing the perfect cake, either dislike the flavour or not liking the design of the cake. Hence, Cakerie provides a special feature of allowing customers to customize their cake. We do understand some customers have difficulty in making decision. So, we also provide a randomized feature. The randomized cake will create a sense of surprise to the customers. However, the randomized cake is based on the top selected cakes of our store to tailor to popular taste. Not only does this online Cakerie website provide a better user experience to customers in ordering cakes, but also help in automatically updating all the relevant information into our database.
